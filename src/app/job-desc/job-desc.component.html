Quality Assurance Co-op


Regression testing for multiple clients whenever a new version is scheduled to be released.

Daily tasks

Testing tickets related to client reported issues to see if they are legitimate issues and responding to them as appropriate.

Daily tasks

Retesting fixed tickets to confirm the issues have been resolved according to the client’s needs.

UNIQUE ASPECTS OF THE JOB

The interesting juxtaposition of the QA team with respect to the developers and the client made the job very unique: nearly all issues that clients report come by the QA team and we try our best to pass along the client’s concerns while adding on with our own technical knowledge of the product to create the most straightforward product feedback that the devs can then build upon to improve the product, while satisfying the client's needs.
