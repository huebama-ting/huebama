HIGHTLIGHT OF THE JOB

Once again, this combined work term was full of highlights. Aside from being able to work during a time crunch, which I will cover later, I also got to explore a lot of new technologies and worked with great people. I would say the largest highlight this work term was being given the opportunity to visit the company's offices in Windsor as part of a celebration. During the five days I spent in Windsor, I got my first experience of working in an office and seeing (along with hanging out with) all the people that I usually see as a set of pixels in a screen in the flesh, so there were many cool moments to be had! That definitely makes this the highlight of the job for me!
