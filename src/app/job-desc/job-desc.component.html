UNIQUE ASPECTS OF THE JOB

The interesting juxtaposition of the QA team with respect to the developers and the client made the job very unique: nearly all issues that clients report come by the QA team and we try our best to pass along the client’s concerns while adding on with our own technical knowledge of the product to create the most straightforward product feedback that the devs can then build upon to improve the product
