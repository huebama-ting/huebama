COMPANY DESCRIPTION

Camis

Camis is a company based out of Guelph, Ontario that develops web and desktop solutions for all things camping related, from reservations online to reservations via a call center to point of sale in park. Founded in 1979, they're a key player in the end to end camping services niche. Major clients include Ontario Parks (provincal parks), Parks Canada (national parks) and Michigan Parks (state parks).
